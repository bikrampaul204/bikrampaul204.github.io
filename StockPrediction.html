Project Description

Prediction System

For accurate prediction both fundamental and technical factors were considered, i.e. historical stock records, sentiments based on news headlines and public sentiments based on social media tweets. The data sources were yahoo finance for historical records, twitter tweets scrapped using Twint and google news scrapped using api.


Natural Language Preprocessing steps such as lowercase conversion, removal of stopwords, url/twitter handles and few more, stemming and lematization were performed on the news headlines and tweets. Sentiment analysis were performed. Recurrent neural network with LSTM and GRU were trained on past 90 days and the model could predict single day to maximum five future days.

Recommendation System
Recommendation Approach 1

Recommendation based on the similarity of sector and industry.

Recommendation Approach 2

Recommendation based on the returns and volatility of stocks of a company. Considering a period of 60 days for computation of the values of return and volatility.

Recommendation Approach 3

Recommendation based on comparison of the on-balance volume and the close value of a stock. The on-balance volume is a statistical measure and calculated by comparing the current volume and the current close price. The trends of the obv and close indicates the possible future movement of the stock.

Recommendation Approach 4

Recommendation based on the moving average of the stock with two sliders one being of 200 past days and 50 past days. The 200 moving average slider provides the indication of the threshold average value of the stock on longer term. The 50 moving average slider provides the indication of the current status of the stock.
